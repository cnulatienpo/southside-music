# THE BAZAAR — Flea Market of Global Sound

## Tone
- messy
- crowded
- multilingual
- sensory overload
- folding tables full of sound

## What Happens Here
- listen to clips
- talk to DeepSeek about traditions
- sample instruments
- explore rhythms & textures
- connect genres across stalls

## Why a Flea Market?
- Culture is lived, not curated
- Genres come from people, communities, accidents, DIY solutions

## Example Stalls
- Chicago House: drum machines humming under tarps
- Detroit Techno: crates of synth manuals and future dreams
- Salsa: congas, cowbells, and block-party brass
- Cumbia: accordion riffs floating over güiro scrape
- Gospel: hand fans, call-and-response, organ pads
- Rembetiko: smoky cafés, bouzouki tremolo, refugee blues
- Hip-Hop: milk-crate setups, quick rhymes, chopped breaks
- Metal: patched tarps, feedback storms, crowd roars
- Afrobeat: polyrhythmic vamps stretching across the aisle
- Bollywood: harmoniums, strings, and a rainbow of filmi hooks
- Industrial: metallic clangs, DIY circuits, dancefloor grit
